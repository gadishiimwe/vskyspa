- Online appointment booking with payment confirmation via MTN Mobile Money

## Services and Packages

### Service Categories
- **Body Massage:** Therapeutic massages including Full Body Relax Massage, Hot Stone Massage, Deep Tissue Massage, and more.

- **Hair Care:** Expert styling, braiding, and hair treatments.
- **Coffee Services:** Premium coffee offerings to complement your spa experience.

### Spa Packages
- **Starter Package:** Ideal for first-time visitors; includes massage, facial, aromatherapy, and more.
- **Premium Package:** Our most popular package with enhanced treatments including manicure, pedicure, and private relaxation suite.
- **Ultimate Package:** The pinnacle of luxury with extended full body massage, deluxe facial, champagne, and personal concierge.

## Pages Overview

### Home
- **Navbar & Footer:** Persistent site-wide navigation and footer components.  
- **Hero Section:** Prominent introductory section with large background images and welcoming text.  
- **Stats Section:** Animated counters displaying key metrics like "Happy Clients," "Years Experience," "Expert Staff," and "Awards Won."  
- **Services Section:** Highlights of main services offered by V&SKY SPA (implemented as a separate component).  
- **Why Choose Us Section:** Features critical selling points such as Premium Products, Expert Therapists, Safety standards, and Personalized Care.  
- **Testimonial Highlight:** Spotlight testimonial with client image and feedback displayed on a luxury spa background.  
- **Special Offers:** Section showcasing limited-time discounts and packages with booking buttons.  
- **Call to Action:** Invitation buttons to book appointments or explore services.

### Booking
- **Navbar & Footer:** Standard site navigation and footer.  
- **Hero Section:** Page title and brief guidance about scheduling an appointment.  
- **Booking Form:** Two-column layout:  
  - Left column with user inputs for Name, Email, Phone, Service selection, Time slot, and Special Notes.  
  - Right column with a calendar date picker restricting past dates.  
- **Payment Confirmation Popup:** Modal dialog showing mobile money payment instructions and "I Paid" confirmation button.  
- **Why Book With Us Section:** Outlines benefits such as Easy Scheduling, Expert Care, and Satisfaction Guarantee.

### Pricing
- **Navbar & Footer:** Navigation consistency.  
- **Hero Section:** Overview introducing available packages and pricing.  
- **Individual Services Section:** Displays categorized services like Massage, Skin Care, Hair Care with detailed pricing and durations.  
- **Call to Action:** Prominent button leading to the booking page.

### About
- **Navbar & Footer:** Consistent navigation.  
- **Hero Section:** Intro to V&SKY SPA with background imagery.  
- **Our Story:** Detailed narrative about spa's founding, growth and location.  
- **Our Values:** Showcasing core values such as Passion, Excellence, Vision, and Quality with icons.  
- **Our Difference Section:** Highlights unique strengths including expert therapists, advanced technology, organic products, and VIP treatment.  
- **Mission & Vision:** Separate cards articulating the spa's mission and vision statements.  
- **Join Our Community CTA:** Encourages visitors to book their first appointment.

### Contact
- **Navbar & Footer:** Standard site elements.  
- **Hero Section:** Encouragement to get in touch and contact information overview.  
- **Contact Info Cards:** Phone numbers, emails, physical location, and operation hours with corresponding icons.  
- **Contact Form:** Inputs for first name, last name, email, phone, subject, and message with submission success notification.  
- **Google Maps Embed:** Interactive map showing V&SKY SPA location.  
- **FAQ Section:** Answers to common client questions (booking process, cancellation policy, gift certificates, appointments tips).  
- **Visit Us Section:** Details on location accessibility, hours, parking, and booking suggestions.

### Services
- **Navbar & Footer:** Navigation maintained.  
- **Hero Section:** Introduction to spa services with a background image.  
- **Services Grid:** Cards representing service categories like Body Massage, Skin Care, Hair Care, Coffee Services; each with an icon, description, treatment list, pricing, and call-to-action buttons.  
- **Why Choose Us Section:** Highlights advantages such as expert staff, use of natural products, and peaceful setting.  
- **Assistance CTA:** Offers contact and booking options for help choosing appropriate treatments.

### Gallery
- **Navbar & Footer:** Site navigation framework.  
- **Hero Section:** Inviting visitors to take a virtual tour of spa facilities.  
- **Gallery Grid:** Dynamically generated grid of images from assets folder with hover overlays showing titles and categories.  
- **Booking CTA:** Call to action encouraging visitors to arrange a spa visit.

### Team
- **Navbar & Footer:** Consistent navigation elements.  
- **Hero Section:** Introduction to expert team members.  
- **Team Profiles Grid:** Cards with photos, names, roles, specialties, personal descriptions, and contact/social links.  
- **Join Our Team CTAs:** Multiple invitations and links for career opportunities and applications.

### Testimonials
- **Navbar & Footer:** Standard layout components.  
- **Hero Section:** Introduction to client testimonials.  
- **Stats Section:** Key performance stats including happy clients, average rating, recommendation rate, and years of experience.  
- **Testimonials Grid:** Individual client reviews with star ratings, photos, service badges, and location details.  
- **Final CTA:** Encouragement to book an appointment based on client satisfaction.

```bash
npm run dev       # or yarn dev
```
